Community Outreach

500+ students reached

A Walk for Education

We raised money and distributed over 50 SAT prep books to students in underserved communities, while educating families on college pathways and career readiness.

Lomax Elementary STEM Elective Class

Bi-weekly, our members lead engaging STEM sessions for elementary students—introducing foundational engineering concepts through fun, interactive lessons with robotics.

USF Engineering Expo

We hosted immersive activities like oobleck mixtures, baking soda volcanoes, and a liquid sand exhibit to excite K–12 visitors about STEM at USF's largest outreach event.

Professional Development

10+ company events

Corporate Partner Spotlights

Industry leaders such as Honeywell, WSP, and Accenture host private sessions with our members, sharing career insights and recruiting talent from our chapter.

Corporate Office Tours

Through exclusive visits to company headquarters and facilities, members gain firsthand insight into workplace culture, engineering operations, and early career pathways.

Conference Preparation Series

In the lead-up to FRC and the National Convention, we support members with targeted prep: resumes, attire tips, networking strategy, and goal-setting.

Academic Excellence

380+ active members

Academic Study Halls

Quarterly study halls provide a focused, collaborative space where members can review course material, receive tutoring, and hold each other accountable in the semester.

King High School College Readiness Program

Our members joined a scholar panel for rising seniors, offering real insights on navigating college life, choosing majors, and preparing for post-secondary success.

Graduate School Prep Workshops

Workshops and resources to help members prepare for graduate school applications, exams, and interviews.

Environmental & Service

Multiple service projects yearly

Sunset Beach Clean-Up

Our chapter rolled up our sleeves for a coastal cleanup at Sunset Beach, demonstrating our commitment to environmental stewardship and public service.

Volunteer Day with Metropolitan Ministries

USF NSBE members volunteered with Metropolitan Ministries—serving meals, sorting through clothing donations, and bringing care to families in need.
